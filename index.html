The Problem We Solve

Micro-entrepreneurs are the backbone of their economies, but they face a critical challenge: **fragmented productivity**. They rely on inefficient, disjointed methods—from notebooks for sales records to multiple messaging apps for gig work—leading to wasted time, lost financial data, and a constant cognitive burden that prevents them from scaling their businesses.
